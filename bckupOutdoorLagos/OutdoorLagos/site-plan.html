OutdoorLagos Site Plan

Site Name

OutdoorLagos - Selected to directly communicate the site's focus on outdoor activities in Lagos.

Site Purpose

  • Provide real-time weather information for Lagos
  • Recommend suitable outdoor activities based on current conditions
  • Help users plan outdoor activities with weather-aware scheduling
  • Offer location-specific air quality data

Scenarios

  • What outdoor activities are suitable today given the current weather in Lagos?
  • Which beaches in Lagos are best to visit based on today's weather forecast?
  • What's the best time to visit Lekki Conservation Centre this week?
  • Is the air quality suitable for outdoor dining in Victoria Island today?
