About OutdoorLagos

Enhancing outdoor experiences in Lagos through smart weather-based planning.

Our Mission

OutdoorLagos is dedicated to helping residents and visitors make informed decisions about outdoor activities based on real-time weather conditions. We combine accurate weather data with local knowledge to ensure you get the most out of your Lagos experience.

Weather Data

We use the OpenWeatherMap API to provide accurate, up-to-date weather information for Lagos. Our weather data is updated every 3 hours to ensure you have the most current information for planning your activities.

Local Expertise

Our team consists of local experts who understand Lagos weather patterns and can recommend the best activities for any weather condition. We work closely with local businesses and tour operators to provide accurate, reliable information.

Weather Safety Tips

Rainy Season (April-October)

  • Always carry rain gear
  • Check flood alerts
  • Have indoor backup plans

Dry Season (November-March)

  • Stay hydrated
  • Use sun protection
  • Plan early morning activities
